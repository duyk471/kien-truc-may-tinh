Cái đồng hồ

In most computers, the passage of time is represented by a master clock that delivers a continuous train of alternating signals (alternates continuously between two phases labeled 0–1, low-high, tick-tock, etc) The elapsed time between the beginning of a ‘‘tick’’ and the end of the subsequent ‘‘tock’’ is called cycle. The current clock phase (tick or tock) is represented by a binary signal.

Mỗi một cycle thường rơi vào khoảng 1/2 nanosecond
